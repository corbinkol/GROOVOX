GROOVOX beta

Jukebox interface for Spotify

Stop people from messing with your account during a party. GROOVOX provides a simple, jukebox style, interface for your guests with all the non-essential features stripped away. With Spotify Premium integration, your guests have full access to Spotify's library without destroying your personal playlists.

IMPORTANT STEPS:

You must click 'remember me' on the Spotify login so that GROOVOX can stay logged in throughout your session.

You must click 'always allow' allow the login popup to auto-refresh auth tokens.

You must have Spotify Premium

Start up the jukebox!

Runs best on Chrome or Firefox
